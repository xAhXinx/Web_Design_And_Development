FAQ

1. How do I make a purchase?
  1. Click ADD TO CART button on the product you want.
  2. You can click the EXPLORE MORE button if you want to get more information about the product.
  3. Continue to scroll through the page if you want to check out more product.
  4. Click at the SHOPPING CART button on the right top corner if you wish to make payment.
  5. You'll reach the PAYMENT page and you have to fill in the PAYMENT FORM in order to make payment.
  6. Click PAY NOW to complete the order.

2. Can I cancel the existing order I made ?
Yes. HOWEVER ,cancellation only can be done if the order has not been shipped out.

3. Can I change my shipping address?
Yes. Please email us for changing the shipping address. HOWEVER , changing shipping address can only be applicable if the order has not been ship.

4. How long does the delivery take?
Maximum 7 working days, but we usually ship the order within 3 working days.

5. What if i recieved damaged or missing item?
Please send an image of the damaged item to our customer service within 48 hours. We will look into the matter seriously and give you the best response as soon as possible. HOWEVER,we will not entertain any complaints about damaged or missing items after 48 hours.

6. Can I change the color variant or size of the product after purchasing?
Yes you can BUT only applicable before the order has been shipped.

7. Do you ship outside Malaysia?
Unfortunately NO , we only ship within Malaysia now.

8. I still have doubts!
Sorry that we can't answer your question.You can contact us through email or WhatsApp.
